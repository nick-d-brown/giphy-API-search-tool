Gifs for your Friends

The only Gif button generator that creates gifs for you to send to your friends!

Simply click on a preset button, or add your own button to click. To do this, start by typing it in the search field the your reaction to send to your friend, then click submit. Your button will appear with the other buttons. When you click on any of the buttons they will select a set of 10 gifs from the GIPHY website for you to view. Click on the Image to activate the gif. Above each of the images is a rating for the gif so you don't accidentally start a gif war by sending the wrong gif to your friend at work (whoops!)
